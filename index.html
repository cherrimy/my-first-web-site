Welcome

The World Wide Web is a space where Internet-connected users can share information with each other. It's also called WWW or W3 for short, and it's most commonly called the Web. Although it is widely used in the same sense as the Internet, the Web is just one popular service on the Internet. But now, the Internet and the Web are the largest parts of the Internet, as they are used interchangeably.
